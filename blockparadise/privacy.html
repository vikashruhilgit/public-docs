3. How we use information

To run and improve the game, save your progress, deliver rewarded ads you choose to watch, process purchases, fix crashes, and keep leaderboards/events fair. We do not use your data for purposes incompatible with these.

4. Data retention

On-device data persists until you delete it (see "Your rights"). Diagnostic/analytics data, when enabled, is retained only as long as needed for the purpose described, per the relevant provider's retention policy.

5. Your rights (access & deletion — GDPR / CCPA)

You can delete all of your on-device data at any time directly in the app: Settings → Data → Delete my data. This erases your local profile, scores, progress, and entitlements on the device. To request access to, or deletion of, any data held off-device (where applicable), contact us at the address below and we will respond within the period required by applicable law.

6. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

The app presents a neutral age screen and limits data collection and personalized advertising for users under the applicable age of digital consent (13 in the US; up to 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below that age beyond what is necessary to run the game.
